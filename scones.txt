Scones

4 cups flour
3/4 cup white sugar
7 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1/2 cup shortening, butter or margarine
1 1/4 cup milk
vanilla flavouring
(optional) 2 lemons, zested
(optional) 2 tsp. lemon poppyseeds

Mix all dry ingredients and cut in shortening or butter.  Add in assorted dry
flavour ingredients.  Mix milk and vanilla flavouring together and pour over
dry ingredients.  Mix with fork until all liquid is absorbed.  Kneed until
dough is soft.  Roll into a 1/2" thick slab and cut into shapes.

Bake for 11 minutes at 425 F (non-convection oven).
