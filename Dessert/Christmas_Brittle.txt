Christmas Brittle

1 sleeve saltine crackers (maybe more)
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup butter
2 cups chocolate chips
Sprinkles (optional)


1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and line a cookie sheet (15x10‚Äù) with parchment
paper (or alum foil) and arrange the crackers into a single layer, salted side
down.
2. Combine the sugar and butter over medium/low heat, stirring constantly.
3. Bring to a boil and allow to boil for 3 minutes without stirring.
4. Pour the hot sugar mixture over the crackers and bake for 4 minutes.
5. Remove from the oven and quickly sprinkle with chocolate chips.
6. Place back into the oven for one minute.
7. Remove and use a spatula to spread the chocolate evenly, add sprinkles if you
wish.
8. Let cool for about 10 mins and place in the fridge to set for one hour.
9. Break apart and enjoy!
10. Store in an airtight container at room temperature or in the fridge for 10-14
days.
