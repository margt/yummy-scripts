Banana Bread

1/4 cup oil
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
4 ripe bananas, mashed
2 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Beat oil and sugar.  Add eggs and bananas.  Beat well.  Add dry ingredients,
milk and vanilla extract.  Mix well.  Pour into greased pan.  Bake at 350F for
about 1 hour.
