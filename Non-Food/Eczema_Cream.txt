Eczema cream

1/4cup oats
3/4cup coconut oil
several drops of rosemary oil
2 tbsp olive oil.

Finely grind oats to powder, over low heat melt coconut oil in pan until
liquid.  Add few drops of rosemary oil, add ground oats and mix until blended.
Pour olive oil into mixture until blended.  Remove from heat and pour into jar.
Let harden.
